About Me

Here's a little bit of information to get to know me a little better

I am a full stack developer with experience building full stack web and mobile applications specializing in front end technologies. I have a strong passion for development and the process involved in building full-stack applications. I have a strong eye for simple and elegant designs which allows the user to easily navigate throughout the application. I am a quick learner and can quickly adapt to new concepts in a short period of time. When I am not programming, you can find me either fishing or playing video games. Video games have played a big part of my life and are what ultimately lead me to become a developer. I plan to learn game development, but not until I master the art of web development.
